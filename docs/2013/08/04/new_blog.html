A wild blog appears!

Aug 4 2013

My old blog at trouble.net.au seems to have died - and it hadn't been updated for ages.

So I'm starting a new one here, at http://blog.korny.info - there isn't much here right now, but I will work on that soon.

The blog is being built with middleman for it's nifty static templating magic, neat bourbon for Bourbon's nice scss addons, and the Neat grid system. And some starting structure from the Middleman Bourbon template by Kuldar

Update: I ended up switching to kraken rather than neat bourbon - I wanted something very simple, because really I just wanted to get text out there rather than fiddle with things.
